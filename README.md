## 🔒 The Local-First Philosophy

> [!IMPORTANT]  
> **100% Offline. 100% Private.**  
> Your financial data is one of your most intimate digital footprints. Unlike modern budgeting apps that harvest, sync, and sell your transaction history, **buckflo** is built on a local-first architecture. All records are stored securely on your device using a high-performance local database. **Your money stays your business.**

## What buckflo Does

### 1. Dual-Wallet Spending & Savings

- **The Spending Wallet:** Your daily hub for flexible lifestyle expenses. Automatically calculated leftovers keep you grounded in what you can actually afford.
- **The Savings Wallet:** A dedicated space for your long-term dreams. Keep your savings isolated from daily temptations, complete with automated pacing and visual milestone indicators.
- **One-Tap Transfers:** Effortlessly move money between spending and savings as your goals and priorities evolve.

### 2. Predictive Analytics

- **Exhaustion Projection:** By analyzing your current month's spending velocity, buckflo predicts the **exact calendar day** you will run out of money if your behavior doesn't change.
- **Tone-Adaptive Trends & Insights:** Get real-time, animated, narrative breakdowns comparing your week-over-week and month-over-month trajectories, with a tone that celebrates savings and cautions against spending spikes.
- **Same-Day Benchmarks:** Fairly compare your spending up to _today_ with the exact same date of the previous month to see if you are on track.

### 3. Committed Expenses & Autopay

- **Committed Expenses System:** Exclude fixed bills (rent, utilities, loans) from your daily flexible budget. Track due days, and mark bills as paid to auto-log them.
- **Subscription Autopay Engine:** Register subscriptions with custom cycles (weekly, monthly, 3-monthly, 6-monthly, yearly). buckflo silently processes and logs due subscriptions on app launch, offering easy one-tap prompts to pause autopay or skip a billing cycle.
- **Month Carry-Forward:** Seamlessly transition to a new month by carrying forward leftover balances and cloning previous category budgets in a single tap.

## 💾 Data Portability & Backup

> **You Own Your Data**  
> All your financial history is stored in a single encrypted JSON file on your device. You can export it, back it up, or move it to another device. We can't sell it because we never see it.

- **CSV Utility:** Bulk import transactions from your bank statements or export data ranges.
- **Full Database Backup:** Export your entire setup, profile config, and history into a single encrypted JSON file to restore on any other device.
- **Nuclear Option:** Wipe all database records from your device in a single tap.
